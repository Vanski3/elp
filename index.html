About El Pollo Loco

El Pollo Loco is a 2D action game in which a Mexican hero fights chicks, chickens and a giant chicken boss. Jump on chicks and chickens and throw Tabasco bottles at the boss to win. Beat the boss to emerge victorious!

Have fun!
